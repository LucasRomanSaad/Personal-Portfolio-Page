Jim's Dinner App

Jimmy's Dinner App is a sleek and efficient web application designed for easy food ordering. It allows users to browse through a menu, add items to their order, and view a summary before completing their purchase. The app's responsive design and dark mode feature enhance user experience, making it convenient and visually appealing for customers to place their orders. Buildt with HTML, CSS and vanilla JavaScript.
